I choose Helaman 5:12 because it serves as my personal spiritual anchor whenever life feels overwhelming, uncertain, or full of temptation. The verse doesn't promise that I will escape the storms of life, but it gives me a definitive guarantee that those storms cannot defeat me if my foundation is secure. When Helaman repeats the words "remember, remember," I feel like he is speaking directly to me, reminding me to prioritize the daily habits that connect me to the Savior. Ultimately, this scripture grounds me during difficult transitions because it shifts my focus away from my own weaknesses or changing circumstances, reminding me that my ultimate strength and stability come from building my life upon the rock of Jesus Christ.
